Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of jelly

Steps

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Put the two slices of bread together, with the peanut butter and jelly facing each other.
  4. Cut the sandwich in half, diagonally, and serve.
