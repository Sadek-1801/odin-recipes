Scrumble Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them together with the milk.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Heat a small frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Add a knob of butter to the pan and let it melt.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and cook, stirring constantly, until the eggs are cooked through.
  6. Serve immediately.
