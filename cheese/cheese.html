Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter, softened
  • 1 slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Spread the butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place the cheese on top of one slice of bread.
  3. Put the two slices of bread together, with the buttered sides facing each other.
  4. Heat a small frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Place the sandwich in the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve immediately.
